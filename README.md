Privacy Policy for Soul of a Dog mobile game.

Soul of a Dog Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-05-29

Soul of a Dog does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Soul of a Dog does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

Game progress is stored locally on the user's device.

The game does not require user accounts and does not transmit personal data to external servers.

If this policy changes in future versions, it will be updated accordingly.
